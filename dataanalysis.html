Data Analysis

The data is analyzed as soon as it is available. There will be a little delay while processing and interpreting the data. It will helpful for decision-making. The term analysis involves continuously monitoring the data streams and enabling quick responses, for an example in financial trading, online retails.

Goal

In general, companies have data which contains hidden information. Asking the right questions and analyzing the data, which gives us relevant insights that can be accurate. The goal is to answer the right questions which are arised, which helps the company to make right decisions.

RESULT

Results are presented in the reports with some recommendations. By looking into these reports, we can able to make clear and right decisions, costs can be evaluated.

PROJECT DURATION

Duration of project varies between 4 weeks and 3 months. In order to carry out the project as soon as possible, it is important that the relevant data is collected and should be cleaned. And there should not be any incompleteness.

CASE - HOW CAN I OPTIMIZE THE COOKIE SALES?

For a engineering project I have done an extensive analysis inorder to optimize the cookie sales. They wanted to know How many units have been sold, What is the cost per each cookie and How much Revenue does the cookies made by each month. when the cookie sales is optimized according to the analysis below, turnover can potentially increase by 1.5%.

WHAT IS THE SUM OF UNITS SOLD BY COUNTRY?

Intially the units varies from 200k - 250k. This allows you to analyze the total units sold by each country. The number of units which have sold is decided by the population present in each country.

WHAT IS THE CUMULATIVE PROFIT BY DATE?

I found two situations in the profits process, where the profit varies Product, Country and Dependencies. For each situation I looked at how to improve this.

  1. Sum of Profit BY DATE AND PRODUCT
    Solution: The products like 'Sugar' and 'White Chocolate Macadamia Nut' accounted for the majority of the decrease among Product, offsetting the increase of 'Oatmeal Raisin'. The relative contributions made by 'Oatmeal Raisin', 'Sugar', and 'Chocolate Chip' changed the most.
  2. Sum of Profit BY DATE AND COUNTRY
    Solution: 'Mexico' accounted for the majority of the decrease among Country, offsetting the increase of 'United States'. The relative contributions made by 'Mexico', 'United States', and 'France' changed the most.
WHAT IS THE AGGREGATE PROFIT FOR EACH PRODUCT CATEGORIZED BY MONTH NAME?

Here's the analysis of the 91.67% increase in Sum of Profit between 01 November 2019 and 01 December 2019. In the above analysis 'France' and 'Canada' accounted for the majority of the decrease among Country. The relative contributions made by 'France', 'Germany', and 'Mexico' changed the most.
In case of products, the analysis of the 49.41% decrease in Sum of Profit between 01 October 2019 and 01 November 2019. 'Chocolate Chip' and 'White Chocolate Macadamia Nut' accounted for the majority of the decrease among Product. The relative contributions made by 'Snickerdoodle', 'White Chocolate Macadamia Nut', and 'Chocolate Chip' changed the most.
