Data Analytics

Data analytics stands apart from data analysis by providing insights and forecasting outcomes. While data analysis informs about the present state, data analytics predicts and guides decisions by projecting potential results following specific actions. In essence, data analysis centers on past and present scenarios, whereas data analytics is forward-looking, concentrating on future possibilities.

The case below is an example How to predict trends for Stock Market using LSTM (Long Short Term Memory) and Sentiment Analysis.

Goal

How can we make a recommendation system?. To enhance the understanding of market dynamics by incorporating both historical stock data patterns and sentiment-related information, ultimately improving the precision of stock price forecasts. LSTM with sentiment analysis is most suitabe for predicting stock trends.

RESULT

By using programming and machine learning techniques, I can find notabe improvements in forecast accuracy. By integraing historical stock data patterns and sentiment-related information I can make an algorithm, probability estimation which you can use to make right decisions.

PROJECT DURATION

Duration varies between 2 weeks and 3 months. The project starts form by understanding of the situation and collecting the right data. After collecting the right data, I started to analyze the data and trained the machine. Finally, I made an algorithm (i.e., model) which you can implement in your business.

CASE - HOW ACCURATE IS THE STOCK PRICE PREDICTION MODEL WHEN USING LSTM AND SENTIMENT ANALYSIS?

The accuracy is contingent on various factors, including the quality of data, model architecture, and the effectiveness of sentiment analysis. The model's performance can be assessed using metrics such as Mean Squared Error (MSE), Mean Absolute Error (MAE), or Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE). Regular evaluation against actual stock prices provides a quantitative measure of accuracy. It is crucial to note that the effectiveness of the model may vary based on the specific dataset, the time period considered, and the dynamic nature of market sentiment.

CASE - CAN THE MODEL PROVIDE INSIGHTS INTO WHY CERTAIN SETIMENTS OR PATTERNS INFLUENCE STOCK PRICES?

The stock price prediction model incorporating LSTM and sentiment analysis, while proficient in generating predictions, may have limitations in providing explicit insights into why specific sentiments or patterns influence stock prices. This model operates as a sophisticated computational system, discerning patterns and correlations within historical data to make predictions.
The data is divides into two:
1. 90% as train data.
2. 10% as test data.

CASE - What insights can be gained from the results?

The primary objective of this application is to address the efficiency challenges observed in the current system. In this project, I propose a new system utilizing the LSTM with Sentiment Analysis methodology. - LSTM with Sentiment Analysis.
With the help of predicator, I measured the accuracy of the following approach(es) LSTM algorithm, LSTM with fundamental & technical analysis, and LSTM with sentiment analysis. And proven among three approach(es), that the most suitable analysis for predicting the market price of a stock is LSTM with sentiment analysis [with having lowest RMSE value compared to other two].
This project holds significant value for brokers and investors for investing money in the stock market application (like Web, Mobile application) since it is trained on a huge collection of historical data and build with the following apporach - LSTM with sentiment analysis to predict stock price(s) of companies.
